Companhia de Saneamento Bsico do Estado de So Paulo SABESP provides basic and environmental sanitation services; and supplies treated water on a wholesale basis to residential, commercial, industrial, and governmental customers in the State of So Paulo. Its services comprise water supply, sanitary sewage services, urban rainwater management and drainage services, urban cleaning services, and solid waste management services and related activities, including the planning, operation, maintenance, and commercialization of energy. As of December 31, 2011, it provided water services through 7.5 million water connections to approximately 23.9 million people; and sewage services through 5.9 million sewage connections to approximately 20.5 million people. It operated through 66,389 kilometers of water pipes and mains; and through 45,073 kilometers of sewer lines. The company was founded in 1954 and is headquartered in So Paulo, Brazil.

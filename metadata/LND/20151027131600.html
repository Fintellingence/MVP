Brasilagro Companhia Brasileira de Propriedades Agrcolas, together with its subsidiaries, engages in agriculture, cattle raising, and forestry activities in Brazil. The company operates through three segments: Grains, Sugarcane, and Real Estate. It produces and sells soybean, corn, rice, cotton, and sorghum; and sugarcane and other farming products. The company is also involved in the import and export of agricultural products and inputs; purchase, sale, and/or rental of properties, land, buildings, and real estate in rural and/or urban areas; real estate brokerage; and management of its own and third party assets. It owns 10 farms with an area of 311,582 hectares, including 17,699 leased hectares in 5 Brazilian states. The company was incorporated in 2005 and is headquartered in So Paulo, Brazil.

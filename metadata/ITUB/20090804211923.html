Banco Itau Holding Financeira S.A., together with its subsidiaries, provides various retail and corporate banking services to individuals, small and middle-market companies, and large corporations primarily in Brazil and internationally. The company\'s deposit products include demand, savings, time, and other deposits. Its lending portfolio comprises commercial, real estate, lease financing, government, agricultural, and personal loans. The company also offers credit cards; property and casualty, and life insurance products; automobile financing; private retirement plans; asset management; and capitalization plans, as well as phone and Internet banking, and automated teller machine services. In addition, it provides cash management, treasury, trade, and investment services, as well as trustee, securities brokerage, and financial advisory services; and credit and non-credit products and services. As of December 31, 2007, it operated 2,585 branches, 734 customer site branches, and 23,466 automated teller machines. The company was founded in 1944 and is headquartered in Sao Paulo, Brazil. Banco Itau Holding Financeira S.A. is a subsidiary of Itausa - Investimentos Itau S.A.

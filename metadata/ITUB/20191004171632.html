Itau Unibanco Holding S.A. provides a range of financial products and services to individuals and corporate clients in Brazil and internationally. It operates in three segments: Retail Banking, Wholesale Banking, and Activities with the Market + Corporation. The company accepts demand, savings, and time deposits; and offers personal and payroll, vehicle financing, and residential mortgage loans, as well as loans to large corporate clients, small and medium-sized companies, and individuals. It also provides pension plan and premium bond products, acquiring services, credit cards, and insurance products; private banking, middle-market banking, asset management, capital market, and corporate and investment banking services; and life and personal accident products, automobile and property insurance, credit insurance, and travel insurance, as well as reinsurance products. In addition, the company offers capitalization, investments, foreign exchange and brokerage products, as well as automatic teller machine, telephones, Internet, mobile, and SMS banking services. The company was formerly known as Itau Unibanco Banco Multiplo S.A. and changed its name to Itau Unibanco Holding S.A. in April 2009. The company was founded in 1924 and is headquartered in Sao Paulo, Brazil. Itau Unibanco Holding S.A. operates as a subsidiary of IUPAR - Itau Unibanco Participacoes S.A.

Embraer ? Empresa Brasileira de Aeronautica S.A. designs, develops, manufactures, and sells jet and turboprop aircraft for civil and defense aviation, and aircraft for agricultural use worldwide. It operates in four segments: Commercial Aviation, Executive Aviation, Aviation Services, Defense and Government. The Commercial Aviation segment designs and manufactures various commercial aircraft and jets, as well as provides related support services. The Executive Aviation segment develops and markets executive jets for the fractional ownership companies, charter and air-taxi companies, and high net-worth individuals. The Aviation Services segment provides after-sales customer support services, including field support, spare parts sales and distribution, product warranty and repair administration, engineering support, maintenance engineering, technical publications, and training. The Defense and Government segment designs, develops, integrates, and manufactures a range of defense and government products, including transport, training, light attach, and surveillance aircraft, as well as reconnaissance missions. The company also provides structural components, mechanical and hydraulic systems, and technical services related to the production and maintenance of aerospace material. In addition, it manufactures general aviation propeller aircraft, such as executive jets and crop dusters, as well as offers subcontracting services in connection with the development and manufacture of the landing gear, fuel systems, and fuel tanks for the S-92 Helibus helicopters, as well as leases aircraft. EMBRAER has a strategic alliance with European Aerospace and Defense Group. The company, formerly known as Rio Han Empreendimentos e Participacoes S.A., was founded in 1969. EMBRAER is headquartered in Sao Jose dos Campos, Brazil.

Cosan Limited engages in the energy, food, logistics, infrastructure, and farmland development and management businesses primarily in Brazil, Europe, Latin America, the Middle East, Asia, and North America. The company is involved in the production and marketing of a range of products derived from sugar cane, including raw sugar, and anhydrous and hydrated ethanol; energy cogeneration from sugar cane bagasse; and distribution and marketing of fuels and lubricants under the Shell and Esso brands. In addition, it engages in the retail sale of sugar foods under the Unio and Da Barra brands; and production and distribution of lubricants, as well as provides logistics services, including transportation, port lifting, and storage of sugar. Further, the company is involved in agricultural land and other investment activities. As of May 28, 2012, it operated approximately 4,600 fuel service stations. Cosan Limited was founded in 1936 and is based in So Paulo, Brazil.

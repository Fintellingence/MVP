Cosan Limited engages in energy, food, logistics, infrastructure, and agricultural property management business activities primarily in Brazil, Europe, Latin America, the Middle East, Asia, and North America. The company involves in the production and sale of sugar cane by-products, including raw sugar, fuel, and anhydrous and hydrated ethanol; and activities in the energy cogeneration from sugar cane bagasse. It also distributes and sells fuels through the network of gas stations under the Esso and Shell brands, as well as distributes lubricants. The company operates approximately 4,500 fuel service stations, 550 convenience stores, and 53 distribution depots. In addition, it provides logistics services comprising port lifting, warehousing, storage, transportation, and elevation for sugar products. Further, the company engages in agricultural land and other investment activities. The company was founded in 1936 and is based in Sao Paulo, Brazil.

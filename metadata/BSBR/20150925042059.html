Banco Santander (Brasil) S.A. provides banking products and services in Brazil and internationally. The company offers commercial banking, investment, mortgage, leasing, credit card, and foreign exchange services, as well as various lending and financing services. It also provides buying club management and securities, insurance brokerage, capitalization, and pension plan services. Banco Santander (Brasil) S.A. was founded in 1957 and is headquartered in So Paulo, Brazil.

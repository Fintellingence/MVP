Banco Bradesco S.A. provides a range of banking and financial products and services to individuals; large, mid-sized, and small companies; and local and international corporations and institutions in Brazil and internationally. The company\'s deposit products include demand deposits, time deposits, savings accounts, and federal funds. Its loan portfolio comprises credit and debit cards, sureties and guarantees, letters of credit, advances of credit card receivables, debentures, promissory notes, co-obligations in loan assignments, real estate receivables certificates, and rural loans, as well as mortgages for the construction and acquisition of properties. The company also provides life and accident, health, automobile, property, and casualty insurance products; pension plans; capitalization bonds; and investment funds, as well as asset management, leasing, investment banking, and loan collection and recovery services. As of December 31, 2013, it operated through a network of 8,260 branches and PAs in Brazil; 3 overseas branches; and 33,464 ATM machines. Banco Bradesco S.A. was founded in 1943 and is headquartered in Osasco, Brazil.

Gol Linhas Aereas Inteligentes S.A. provides regular and non-regular flight transportation services for passengers, cargoes, and mailbags in Brazil and internationally. The company operates in two segments, Flight Transportation and Smiles Loyalty Program. As of December 31, 2016, it operated a fleet of 130 aircraft, which included 96 aircraft under operating leases and 34 aircraft under finance leases. It also develops and manages third party\'s customer loyalty program, as well as sells redemption rights of awards related to the loyalty program. The company has a strategic partnership with Delta Air Lines, Inc. Gol Linhas Aereas Inteligentes S.A. was founded in 2001 and is based in Sao Paulo, Brazil.

Ultrapar Holdings, Inc., through its subsidiaries, distributes liquefied petroleum gas to residential, commercial, and industrial customers primarily in the south, southeast, and northeast regions of Brazil. It also engages in the production and sale of chemicals, such as ethylene oxide, ethylene glycols, ethanolamines, glycol ethers, and methyl-ethyl-ketone, as well as specialty chemicals that are used for textiles, foods, cosmetics, detergents, agricultural chemicals, oil field chemicals, leather, paints, and varnishes industries. In addition, the company provides integrated road transport, storage, handling and logistics planning services for chemicals and fuels. Further, it distributes fuels/lubricants and related products in the south and southeast regions of Brazil, as well as engages in oil refining. The company was founded in 1937 and is headquartered in Sao Paulo, Brazil.

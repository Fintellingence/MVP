Telefnica Brasil S.A. provides fixed-line telecommunications services to residential and commercial customers in Brazil. The company offers voice services, including activation, monthly subscription, measured service, and public telephones; intraregional, interregional, and international long-distance voice services; data services comprising broadband services; mobile value added services; Pay TV services through direct to home satellite technology, IPTV, and cable; network services, such as rental of facilities, as well as other services, including extended maintenance, caller identification, voice mail, cell phone blockers, computer support, antivirus for Internet service subscribers, and posto informtico; and digital services, such as financial services, M2M, e-health solutions, security, video, and advertising. It also provides multimedia communication services, which include audio, data, voice and other sounds, images, and texts and other information. In addition, the company offers interconnection services to mobile service providers and other fixed telecommunications companies using its network; and sells wireless devices and accessories. Further, it provides Internet access service; and telecommunications solutions and IT support to industries, such as retail, manufacturing, services, financial institutions, government, etc. The company offers its solutions through its stores, dealers, recharge channels, telemarketing call centers, an online store, physical channels, and person-to-person sales. As of December 31, 2013, the company\'s telephone network included 10,750 thousand fixed-lines in service; 77,240 thousand mobile accesses; 3,922 thousand broadband clients; and 641 thousand Pay TV clients. The company was formerly known as Telecomunicaes de So Paulo S.A. TELESP and changed its name to Telefnica Brasil S.A. in October 2011 in connection with the merger with Vivo Participaes S.A. Telefnica Brasil S.A. is headquartered in So Paulo, Brazil.
